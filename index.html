    An Equinox is the instant of time when the plane of Earth's equator passes through the geometric center of the Sun's disk. In simpler terms, if you are at the equator, the sun in directly above, and the amount of daytime to night time is theoretically equal.

    A Solstice is an event that occurs when the Sun appears to reach its most northerly or southerly excursion relative to the celestial equator on the celestial sphere. In simpler terms, depending on which side of the equator, the day time will be longer portion of the day than nighttime in the Summer Solstice, and the nighttime will be longer than the daytime in the Winter Solstice.

    Seasons are an affect of the 23.5deg tilt of our rotation and with an understanding of these seasons, humans would create civilization.
